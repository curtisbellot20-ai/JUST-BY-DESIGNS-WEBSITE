General Questions

What is Just By Designs? +
Just By Designs is South Florida's premier luxury event draping and transformation company. We specialize in creating breathtaking event environments through custom fabric installations, ceiling drapery, backdrops, stage design, and lighting integration. We serve Broward, Miami-Dade, and Palm Beach County.
How long has Just By Designs been in business? +
Just By Designs has been transforming South Florida events for over 15 years. In that time, we've completed more than 500 events — from intimate weddings in Hollywood, Florida to grand corporate galas at the most prestigious venues in Miami and Boca Raton.
What types of events does Just By Designs serve? +
We serve virtually every type of celebration: weddings, quinceañeras, sweet 16s, birthday parties, corporate galas, charity fundraisers, themed events, graduations, holiday parties, product launches, and any occasion that deserves a spectacular environment.
Does Just By Designs provide a free consultation? +
Yes! We offer consultations to discuss your event vision, venue, budget, and design preferences. During this conversation, we get to know your specific needs and begin developing a custom plan. Contact us at (954) 839-7173 or through our website to schedule.
Is Just By Designs a local South Florida company? +
Absolutely. Just By Designs is a proudly local South Florida company, based in Broward County. We have deep roots in this community and have worked at hundreds of venues throughout Broward, Miami-Dade, and Palm Beach County over our 15+ years in business.
How do I see examples of your work? +
You can view our portfolio on our gallery page and on our social media — Instagram @justbydesigns and Facebook at Justbydesignllc. Our gallery includes weddings, corporate events, themed celebrations, and social gatherings across South Florida.

Services Questions

What services does Just By Designs offer? +
We offer six signature services: Wedding Transformations, Ceiling Drapery, Luxury Event Drapery, Backdrops & Stage Design, Lighting Integration, and Custom Event Design. Each service can be customized to your specific vision, venue, and budget.
Can Just By Designs transform any type of venue? +
Yes. We have the expertise and equipment to transform virtually any venue — hotel ballrooms, tented events, outdoor venues, country clubs, warehouses, backyards, churches, and more. Our team assesses each venue individually to determine the best approach.
Do you do ceiling drapery installations? +
Ceiling drapery is one of our signature specialties. We create everything from classic gathered swag treatments to elaborate starlight canopies and dramatic starburst designs. We handle all rigging and structural requirements safely and professionally.
Can you integrate lighting with the drapery? +
Absolutely. We offer lighting integration as a standalone service and as an add-on to any drapery installation. We work with fairy lights, uplighting, pin spots, LED systems, and programmable color-changing effects to elevate every installation.
Do you create custom backdrops for photo opportunities? +
Yes. Custom backdrops are a major part of what we do. We create fabric photo backdrops, ceremony backdrops, stage backdrops, and photo booth installations in any size, shape, or design — perfect for capturing memories at any event.
Can you handle large-scale corporate events? +
Absolutely. We have extensive experience with large-scale corporate events throughout South Florida, including galas, conferences, product launches, and award ceremonies. We work with corporate event planners and handle everything from concept to installation.
Do you work with other event vendors and planners? +
Yes, we regularly collaborate with event planners, florists, lighting companies, caterers, and venue coordinators throughout South Florida. We're experienced at working as part of a larger event team and pride ourselves on seamless coordination.

Wedding Questions

Can Just By Designs transform my entire wedding venue? +
Yes! Wedding venue transformation is our specialty. We can handle every fabric element of your wedding — ceremony backdrop, aisle treatments, ceiling drapery, reception walls, sweetheart table, and more. We create a cohesive environment from ceremony through reception.
How far in advance should I book for my wedding? +
We recommend booking as early as possible, ideally 6-12 months in advance for weddings. Popular South Florida wedding dates fill up quickly. However, we do our best to accommodate shorter lead times when our schedule allows — contact us to check availability.
Can you work with my wedding's specific color palette? +
Absolutely. We source fabrics in an enormous range of colors and can custom-match virtually any palette. Whether you're working with classic ivory and gold, modern navy and blush, or a bold jewel-toned palette, we'll source the perfect fabrics for your vision.
Do you do outdoor weddings in South Florida? +
Yes. We have extensive experience with outdoor weddings across South Florida, including tented events, beach ceremonies, garden weddings, and open-air venues. We know how to work with South Florida's climate and create installations that are both beautiful and structurally sound.
Can you work at any wedding venue in South Florida? +
We have worked at hundreds of venues throughout Broward, Miami-Dade, and Palm Beach County, including major hotel ballrooms, country clubs, private estates, and unique event spaces. We always do a venue walkthrough to ensure our designs work perfectly with the specific space.

Booking Questions

How do I book Just By Designs for my event? +
The easiest ways to book are to call us at (954) 839-7173, fill out our contact form on our website, or reach out via Instagram @justbydesigns or Facebook. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your event and begin building your custom design plan.
What information do I need to provide when booking? +
To get started, it's helpful to share your event date, venue name and location, type of event, approximate guest count, color palette preferences, and any design inspiration images you have. The more details you can share, the better we can tailor our proposal to your vision.
Is a deposit required to reserve my date? +
Yes, we require a deposit to secure your event date and begin the design process. The deposit amount and payment terms will be discussed during your consultation and outlined in your contract. Contact us for current deposit requirements.
What is your cancellation policy? +
Our cancellation policy is outlined in detail in each client contract. We understand that circumstances change and work with clients as fairly as possible. Please review the contract terms and reach out to discuss any concerns about cancellation policies.
Do you have a minimum order requirement? +
We serve events of varying sizes and budgets. Contact us to discuss your specific event needs and we'll let you know how we can best serve you. We are committed to providing value at every price point while maintaining our signature luxury standards.

Pricing Questions

How much does event drapery cost in South Florida? +
Pricing for event drapery varies widely based on the scope of the installation, the size of the venue, the type of fabrics and techniques used, and the complexity of the design. We provide custom quotes for every event. Contact us for a personalized estimate for your specific event.
Does Just By Designs offer packages? +
We offer both customizable packages and fully bespoke designs. During your consultation, we'll discuss your vision and budget and present options that work for you. Whether you're looking for a focused backdrop installation or a complete venue transformation, we can build a solution around your needs.
Are travel fees included for events outside Broward County? +
We serve Broward, Miami-Dade, and Palm Beach County as our primary service areas. Travel within these three counties is typically included in our pricing. For events outside these areas, additional travel fees may apply — please ask during your consultation.
Do you offer any discounts for non-profit or charity events? +
We believe in giving back to the South Florida community. We consider requests from non-profit and charitable organizations on a case-by-case basis. Contact us to discuss your event and we'll do our best to find a way to work within your budget.

Service Area Questions

Which cities in Broward County does Just By Designs serve? +
We serve all of Broward County, including Fort Lauderdale, Hollywood, Pembroke Pines, Miramar, Coral Springs, Weston, Plantation, Davie, Deerfield Beach, Pompano Beach, and all surrounding communities.
Which cities in Miami-Dade County does Just By Designs serve? +
We serve all of Miami-Dade County, including Miami, Coral Gables, Hialeah, Aventura, Brickell, Coconut Grove, Doral, Miami Beach, Kendall, Pinecrest, and all surrounding communities.
Which cities in Palm Beach County does Just By Designs serve? +
We serve all of Palm Beach County, including Boca Raton, West Palm Beach, Delray Beach, Palm Beach Gardens, Boynton Beach, Wellington, Jupiter, Lake Worth, and all surrounding communities.
Does Just By Designs serve events outside South Florida? +
Our primary service area is the three-county South Florida region — Broward, Miami-Dade, and Palm Beach County. For events outside this area, please contact us to discuss availability and any additional travel requirements. We evaluate out-of-area requests on a case-by-case basis.
