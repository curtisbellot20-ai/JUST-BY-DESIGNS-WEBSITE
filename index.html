What We Do

Crafting Unforgettable Spaces

From intimate celebrations to grand-scale productions, our team brings expertise in drapery design and structural trussing to every project.

01

Custom Drapery

Flowing fabric installations tailored to your venue — ceiling swags, backdrops, wall treatments, and more. Every fold placed with intention.

02

Structural Trussing

Engineered truss systems for stages, lighting rigs, and grand architectural features that make a statement and hold the room.

03

Event Lighting

Ambient uplighting, pin spots, gobo projections, and wash lighting that sculpt atmosphere and set the perfect emotional tone for any occasion.

04

Full Event Design

End-to-end coordination of drapery, trussing, and décor. We orchestrate every element to create a cohesive, stunning environment from concept to teardown.
