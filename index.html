About Just By Designs

Where Every Space Tells a Story

"Before the celebration, before the music, before the guests arrive — there is the atmosphere."

Just By Designs is South Florida's premier luxury event draping company, specializing in transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary environments. Based in Broward County and serving Miami-Dade and Palm Beach County, we bring over 15 years of artistry and expertise to every event.

From intimate weddings to grand corporate galas, our team of skilled artisans crafts custom drapery installations, ceiling treatments, backdrops, and complete event environments that leave lasting impressions.
