Our Services

From intimate ceremonies to grand galas, we offer a complete suite of luxury event design services throughout South Florida's Broward, Miami-Dade, and Palm Beach Counties.

Service 01

Wedding Transformations

Your wedding day deserves an environment as extraordinary as your love story. Just By Designs creates complete wedding transformations that turn any venue across South Florida into an ethereal dreamscape.

We work closely with couples throughout Broward, Miami-Dade, and Palm Beach County to understand their unique vision, then bring it to life with master-crafted fabric installations, carefully chosen textiles, and precision installation.

  • Full ceremony and reception drapery
  • Bridal entrance backdrops
  • Sweetheart table treatments
  • Aisle and altar framing
  • Tent and ballroom transformations
  • Complete venue assessment and planning

Service 02

Ceiling Drapery

The ceiling is often the most overlooked canvas in event design — and the most impactful when transformed. Our ceiling drapery installations create dramatic overhead environments that change the entire feeling of a space.

Whether you envision a starlit canopy for a romantic wedding in Fort Lauderdale or a billowing cloud installation for a corporate event in Coral Gables, our team has the expertise and equipment to execute flawlessly.

  • Full room ceiling canopies
  • Swag and ruching techniques
  • Starlight fabric installations
  • Tent liner treatments
  • Low and high ceiling solutions
  • Structural rigging and hardware

Service 03

Luxury Event Drapery

Beyond weddings, we bring the same level of artistry and luxury to every type of event. Corporate galas, quinceañeras, anniversaries, charity fundraisers — every celebration deserves an exceptional environment.

Our luxury event drapery service covers every aspect of fabric design for your event space. We source premium materials and use expert techniques to create environments that reflect the prestige of your occasion.

  • Premium fabric selection and sourcing
  • Wall and pillar treatments
  • Entrance and foyer transformations
  • Lounge area drapery
  • Table and floor-length draping
  • Color-coordinated design planning

Service 04

Backdrops & Stage Design

Every great event needs a focal point — a stunning visual anchor that draws the eye and sets the tone. Our custom backdrop and stage design service creates those unforgettable centerpieces.

From fabric ceremony arches and photobooth backdrops to full stage transformations for corporate presentations in Boca Raton or Miami, we design and install backdrops that make every moment more memorable.

  • Ceremony arch and altar backdrops
  • Photo opportunity backdrops
  • Stage and presentation backdrops
  • Balloon and floral integration
  • Custom shaped and sized installations
  • Brand-forward corporate backdrops

Service 05

Lighting Integration

Fabric and light are natural partners. When combined expertly, they create an atmosphere that simply cannot be achieved with either element alone. Our lighting integration service elevates every drapery installation to its full potential.

We work with pin spotting, uplighting, fairy lights, and LED systems to illuminate our fabric installations in ways that transform the mood throughout the event — from ceremony to reception to dancing.

  • Fairy light and string light integration
  • LED uplighting coordination
  • Gobo and pin spot placement
  • Programmable color-changing effects
  • Ambient and accent lighting design
  • Day-to-evening transition planning

Service 06

Custom Event Design

Some events require something completely unique — a vision that doesn't fit into any pre-defined category. Our custom event design service is for those clients who dream without limits and expect their environment to match.

Working across all three South Florida counties — Broward, Miami-Dade, and Palm Beach — our design team collaborates deeply with you to conceptualize, plan, and execute a one-of-a-kind event environment from scratch.

  • Full creative consultation and mood boarding
  • Concept development and 3D visualization
  • Theme development and execution
  • Complete installation and breakdown
  • Day-of coordination and supervision
  • Multi-vendor coordination support
